OUR STORY

Made by equestrians, for equestrians.

Ride in Style Equestrian is an apparel brand made for the modern equestrian. With designs that translate seamlessly from street to stable, we strive to help you meet your riding goals with quality equestrian wear that looks good and feels good too.

Outfitting riders from beginner levels to Grand Prix, our mission is to equip all riders to feel your absolute best through both positive energy and mindset, and to help you rise above any challenges that come your way. Based out of southern Ontario, each of our pieces are thoughtfully designed and rigorously tested to ensure our commitment to quality is satisfied.

R.I.S.E.

The name Ride In Style Equestrian not only described the pieces I was creating but the acronym; R.I.S.E. perfectly summed up the values I wanted to instill in the community behind it. To rise up and accomplish dreams, together.

The foundation for Ride In Style Equestrian was built from a desire to create a line of comfortable, versatile pieces that offered a boutique approach to equestrian apparel.

As a rider who grew up competing in the hunter/jumper industry, I was eager to bring my creative vision for street to stable fashion to life. I didn’t know it at first, but what I was building would go far beyond this. I lost my father in 2020 and that year brought many internal battles, in addition to the struggles many of us faced during that time. Searching for a positive outlet, I leaned into designing pieces that made me feel good to wear.

I spent countless hours creating a collection built on the principles of style, comfort, and quality because I believe clothing that helps you feel your best truly allows you to be your best. I focused on embracing a positive mindset and practicing gratitude to help get me through this incredibly difficult time in my life, and I wanted what I was building to reflect this.

Creating Ride In Style Equestrian proved to me that I could rise above the most difficult period of my life to create something I had once only dreamed of. It’s my hope that this community will help rise you up to do the same.
